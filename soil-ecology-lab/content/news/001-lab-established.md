## 中文

2025年7月，刘华清博士和杨佳慧博士正式加入广西大学农学院，成立土壤生态与水土保持课题组。课题组聚焦植物多样性与土壤水碳耦合、土壤侵蚀与水土保持等前沿方向，欢迎有志青年加入！

## English

In July 2025, Dr. Huaqing Liu and Dr. Jiahui Yang officially joined the College of Agriculture at Guangxi University, establishing the Soil Ecology and Conservation Lab. The lab focuses on plant diversity–soil water-carbon coupling, soil erosion and conservation, and welcomes aspiring students to join!
