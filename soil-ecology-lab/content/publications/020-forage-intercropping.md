## 中文摘要
本研究探讨了不同饲草间作方式对产量和水分利用的影响。
### 研究亮点
- 比较不同饲草间作方式的增产节水效应
- 合理间作模式实现增产与节水双重效益
- 分析了间作方式对水分利用效率的影响
- 为旱区饲草种植制度优化提供参考
## English Summary
This study explored the effects of different forage intercropping patterns on yield increase and water-saving.
### Highlights
- Compared yield and water-saving effects of different forage intercropping patterns
- Rational intercropping achieved dual benefits of yield increase and water saving
- Analyzed intercropping effects on water use efficiency
- Provides references for optimizing forage planting in arid regions
