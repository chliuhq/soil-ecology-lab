## 中文摘要
本研究提出了利用InSAR技术监测土壤侵蚀的创新方法。通过合成孔径雷达干涉测量技术，实现了对土壤侵蚀过程的高精度、大范围定量监测，为遥感技术在水土保持领域的应用开辟了新途径。

### 研究亮点
- 首次将InSAR技术系统应用于土壤侵蚀监测
- 实现了毫米级精度的地表形变检测
- 大范围定量监测突破了传统方法的空间限制
- 为遥感水土保持监测开辟了新技术路径

## English Summary
This study proposes innovative methods for monitoring soil erosion using InSAR technology. Through synthetic aperture radar interferometry, high-precision and large-scale quantitative monitoring of soil erosion processes was achieved, opening new avenues for remote sensing applications in soil conservation.

### Highlights
- First systematic application of InSAR for soil erosion monitoring
- Achieved millimeter-level surface deformation detection
- Large-scale quantitative monitoring overcame spatial limitations of traditional methods
- Opens new technical pathways for remote sensing in soil conservation
