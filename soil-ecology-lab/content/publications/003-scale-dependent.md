## 中文摘要

本研究揭示了树种多样性对土壤抗侵蚀能力的尺度依赖效应。在不同空间尺度上，树种多样性对土壤侵蚀抵抗力的影响机制存在显著差异，为多尺度森林生态系统管理和水土保持策略制定提供了新视角。

### 研究亮点
- 基于全球数据集分析树种多样性与土壤抗蚀性的关系
- 多样性效应在样地和区域尺度上表现出显著差异
- 功能多样性比物种丰富度对抗蚀性的解释力更强
- 为多尺度森林管理和水土保持策略提供了新框架

## English Summary

This study reveals scale-dependent effects of tree diversity on soil erosion resistance. The mechanisms by which tree diversity influences soil erosion resistance vary significantly across spatial scales, offering new perspectives for multi-scale forest ecosystem management and conservation strategies.

### Highlights
- Analyzed tree diversity–soil erosion resistance relationships using global datasets
- Diversity effects showed significant differences between plot and regional scales
- Functional diversity explained erosion resistance better than species richness
- Provides a new framework for multi-scale forest management and conservation
