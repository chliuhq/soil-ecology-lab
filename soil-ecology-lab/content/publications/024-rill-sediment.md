## 中文摘要
本研究探究了黄绵土坡面细沟水流输沙能力对地表冲刷流量的响应规律。
### 研究亮点
- 探究细沟输沙能力对冲刷流量的响应规律
- 不同流量条件下输沙特征存在显著差异
- 建立了流量-输沙能力的定量关系
- 为黄土区侵蚀预报模型改进提供参数支持
## English Summary
This study investigated the response of rill flow sediment transport capacity to surface scouring discharge in loess soil.
### Highlights
- Investigated rill sediment transport response to scouring discharge
- Sediment transport characteristics varied significantly under different flows
- Established quantitative discharge-transport capacity relationships
- Provides parameter support for erosion prediction models in loess regions
