## 中文摘要
本研究调查了青藏高原多年冻土区热融滑塌事件后的土壤性质变化。热融作用引发的质量运动显著改变了土壤理化性质和碳氮储量，揭示了气候变暖背景下冻土退化对土壤生态系统的深远影响。

### 研究亮点
- 调查青藏高原热融滑塌后土壤性质变化
- 热融事件显著改变了土壤理化性质和碳氮储量
- 揭示了冻土退化对土壤生态系统的深远影响
- 为气候变暖下冻土区生态风险评估提供依据

## English Summary
This study investigated soil property changes following a thaw-induced mass movement event in the Qinghai-Tibetan Plateau permafrost region. Thaw-induced mass movements significantly altered soil physicochemical properties and carbon-nitrogen stocks, revealing the profound impacts of permafrost degradation on soil ecosystems under climate warming.

### Highlights
- Investigated soil property changes after thaw-induced mass movement on QTP
- Thaw events significantly altered soil physicochemical properties and C-N stocks
- Revealed profound impacts of permafrost degradation on soil ecosystems
- Provides basis for ecological risk assessment in permafrost regions under warming
