## 中文摘要
本研究系统研究了寒区草地冻融诱发滑坡的发生机制。
### 研究亮点
- 系统揭示寒区草地冻融滑坡的发生机制
- 冻融循环改变土壤力学性质和水分状态
- 草地根系对滑坡稳定性的影响具有双重性
- 为寒区草地灾害防治提供了重要认识
## English Summary
This study systematically investigated the mechanisms of freeze-thaw induced landslides on grassland in cold regions.
### Highlights
- Systematically revealed mechanisms of freeze-thaw landslides on grassland
- Freeze-thaw cycles altered soil mechanical properties and moisture conditions
- Grassland root systems have dual effects on landslide stability
- Provides important insights for hazard prevention in cold region grasslands
