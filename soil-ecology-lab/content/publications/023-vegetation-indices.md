## 中文摘要
本文系统综述了54种植被指数的研究进展，涵盖了各类植被指数的原理、适用条件和应用领域。
### 研究亮点
- 系统综述54种植被指数的原理与应用
- 涵盖宽带、窄带和高光谱植被指数
- 分析各指数的适用条件和局限性
- 为遥感植被监测中指数选择提供全面参考
## English Summary
This review systematically summarized research progress on 54 vegetation indices.
### Highlights
- Systematic review of 54 vegetation indices' principles and applications
- Covers broadband, narrowband, and hyperspectral vegetation indices
- Analyzed applicable conditions and limitations of each index
- Provides comprehensive reference for VI selection in remote sensing
