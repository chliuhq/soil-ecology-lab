## 中文摘要
本研究探究了禾豆间作系统中水分和根系分隔对牧草氮素吸收利用及转移的影响机制。
### 研究亮点
- 水分和根系分隔实验揭示氮素互作机制
- 间作促进了豆科向禾本科的氮素转移
- 根系交互是种间氮素互作的关键途径
- 为间作体系养分管理优化提供科学依据
## English Summary
This study investigated the effects of water and root separation on nitrogen uptake, utilization, and transfer in grass-legume intercropping systems.
### Highlights
- Water and root separation experiments revealed nitrogen interaction mechanisms
- Intercropping promoted nitrogen transfer from legumes to grasses
- Root interaction is the key pathway for interspecific nitrogen exchange
- Provides scientific basis for optimizing nutrient management in intercropping
