## 中文摘要

本研究揭示了秸秆还田处理对黄土坡面降雨径流的水动力学机制。秸秆掺入改变了坡面水流的流态和能量分布，有效降低了径流侵蚀力，为黄土高原水土保持措施优化提供了理论支撑。

### 研究亮点
- 系统分析秸秆还田对坡面径流水动力学的影响
- 秸秆掺入显著改变了水流流态和雷诺数
- 径流能量分布的变化降低了侵蚀动力
- 为黄土高原水土保持措施优化提供理论支撑

## English Summary

This study reveals the hydrodynamic mechanism of rainfall runoff on loess slopes treated with incorporated straw. Straw incorporation altered flow regime and energy distribution, effectively reducing runoff erosivity and providing theoretical support for optimizing conservation measures on the Loess Plateau.

### Highlights
- Systematically analyzed straw incorporation effects on slope runoff hydrodynamics
- Straw incorporation significantly altered flow regime and Reynolds number
- Changes in runoff energy distribution reduced erosion dynamics
- Provides theoretical support for optimizing conservation on the Loess Plateau
