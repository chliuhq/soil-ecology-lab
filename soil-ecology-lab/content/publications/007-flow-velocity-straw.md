## 中文摘要
本研究系统探究了小麦秸秆掺入对耕作坡面水流流速的影响规律。结果表明秸秆还田显著降低了坡面流速，改变了水流的水力学特性，为农田坡面水土保持技术的改进提供了实验依据。

### 研究亮点
- 定量测定秸秆掺入对坡面流速的影响
- 秸秆还田显著降低了坡面水流流速
- 建立了秸秆掺入量与流速的定量关系
- 为农田坡面水土保持技术改进提供实验依据

## English Summary
This study systematically investigated the effects of wheat straw incorporation on flow velocity over cultivated soil slopes. Results showed that straw incorporation significantly reduced surface flow velocity and altered hydraulic characteristics, providing experimental evidence for improving farmland slope conservation techniques.

### Highlights
- Quantified straw incorporation effects on slope flow velocity
- Straw incorporation significantly reduced surface flow velocity
- Established quantitative relationships between straw amount and flow velocity
- Provides experimental evidence for improving farmland conservation techniques
