## 中文摘要

本研究发现短期植物混种能够改变土壤有机碳组分和微生物网络特征。混种处理显著影响了土壤碳库的组成结构和微生物群落的互作网络，揭示了植物多样性驱动土壤碳循环的微生物学机制。

### 研究亮点
- 短期混种即可显著改变土壤有机碳组分
- 混种增加了微生物网络的复杂性和稳定性
- 真菌群落对混种的响应比细菌更敏感
- 揭示了多样性驱动碳循环的微生物学机制

## English Summary

This study found that short-term plant mixtures alter soil organic carbon components and microbial network characteristics. Mixed planting significantly affected soil carbon pool composition and microbial community interaction networks, revealing microbiological mechanisms of plant diversity-driven soil carbon cycling.

### Highlights
- Short-term mixtures significantly altered SOC components
- Mixtures increased microbial network complexity and stability
- Fungal communities responded more sensitively to mixtures than bacteria
- Revealed microbiological mechanisms of diversity-driven carbon cycling
