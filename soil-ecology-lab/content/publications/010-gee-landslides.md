## 中文摘要
本研究利用Google Earth Engine和图像融合技术，增强了青藏高原治多县冻融滑坡的检测能力。

### 研究亮点
- 利用GEE平台实现大规模冻融滑坡自动检测
- 多源遥感图像融合显著提高识别精度
- 建立了高寒区滑坡遥感监测技术流程
- 为青藏高原地质灾害预警提供技术支撑

## English Summary
This study enhanced the detection of freeze-thaw induced landslides in Zhidoi county using Google Earth Engine and image fusion.

### Highlights
- Achieved large-scale freeze-thaw landslide detection using GEE platform
- Multi-source image fusion significantly improved identification accuracy
- Established remote sensing monitoring workflow for alpine landslides
- Provides technical support for geological hazard early warning on QTP
