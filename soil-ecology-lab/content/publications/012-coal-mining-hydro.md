## 中文摘要
本研究通过全球荟萃分析，揭示了煤矿开采对流域尺度水文循环的影响规律。
### 研究亮点
- 全球荟萃分析揭示煤矿开采的水文效应
- 煤矿开采系统性改变流域水文循环过程
- 地下水位下降和径流减少是主要影响
- 为矿区水资源管理和生态修复提供科学参考
## English Summary
This global meta-analysis reveals the impacts of coal mining on the hydrologic cycle at watershed scale.
### Highlights
- Global meta-analysis revealing hydrological effects of coal mining
- Coal mining systematically altered watershed hydrologic cycle
- Groundwater decline and runoff reduction are primary impacts
- Provides scientific references for mining area water management and restoration
