## 中文摘要
本研究通过实验探究了融土深度对细沟水流流速的影响。
### 研究亮点
- 实验探究融土深度对细沟流速的影响
- 融土层厚度显著影响水流动力学特征
- 建立了融土深度与流速的定量关系
- 为冻融条件下坡面侵蚀机理提供实验证据
## English Summary
This study experimentally investigated the effects of thawed soil depth on rill flow velocity.
### Highlights
- Experimentally investigated thawed soil depth effects on rill flow velocity
- Thawed soil thickness significantly affected flow dynamics
- Established quantitative relationships between thaw depth and flow velocity
- Provides experimental evidence for slope erosion under freeze-thaw conditions
