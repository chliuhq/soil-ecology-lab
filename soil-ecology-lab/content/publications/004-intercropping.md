## 中文摘要

本研究通过综合分析发现间作种植模式能够显著提高植物水分可利用性和水分利用效率。间作系统通过优化根系空间分布和水分竞争互补关系，实现了增产与节水的双重效益。

### 研究亮点
- 荟萃分析综合全球间作研究数据
- 间作显著提高植物水分可利用性和水分利用效率
- 根系空间互补是间作节水增产的核心机制
- 为旱区农业种植制度优化提供了定量依据

## English Summary

This synthesis demonstrates that intercropping significantly increases plant water availability and water use efficiency. Intercropping systems achieve dual benefits of yield increase and water saving through optimized root spatial distribution and complementary water competition.

### Highlights
- Meta-analysis synthesizing global intercropping research data
- Intercropping significantly increased plant water availability and WUE
- Root spatial complementarity is the core mechanism for water saving and yield increase
- Provides quantitative evidence for optimizing cropping systems in arid regions
