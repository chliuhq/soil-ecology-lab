## 中文摘要
本研究通过全球荟萃分析（1992-2021），系统评估了有机肥和有机-无机肥配施对番茄品质和产量的权衡关系。研究为优化施肥策略、平衡作物产量与品质提供了全球尺度的定量证据。
### 研究亮点
- 全球荟萃分析涵盖1992-2021年研究数据
- 有机-无机配施在产量和品质间取得最佳平衡
- 纯有机肥提升品质但可能降低产量
- 为全球番茄施肥策略优化提供定量依据
## English Summary
This global meta-analysis (1992-2021) systematically evaluated trade-offs between organic and organic-inorganic fertilizer combinations on tomato quality and yield, providing global-scale quantitative evidence for optimizing fertilization strategies and balancing crop yield with quality.
### Highlights
- Global meta-analysis covering 1992-2021 research data
- Organic-inorganic combinations achieved optimal yield-quality balance
- Pure organic fertilizers improved quality but may reduce yield
- Provides quantitative evidence for global tomato fertilization optimization
