## 中文摘要
本研究揭示了掺沙处理对盐渍土土块崩解和盐分淋洗的影响机理。
### 研究亮点
- 揭示掺沙对盐渍土崩解和淋洗的影响机理
- 掺沙改善了盐渍土的物理结构
- 盐分淋洗效率随掺沙量增加而提高
- 为盐碱地改良技术发展提供理论依据
## English Summary
This study revealed the mechanisms of sand mixing on soil clod disintegration and salt leaching in saline soil.
### Highlights
- Revealed mechanisms of sand mixing on saline soil disintegration and leaching
- Sand mixing improved physical structure of saline soil
- Salt leaching efficiency increased with sand mixing rate
- Provides theoretical basis for saline-alkali land improvement
