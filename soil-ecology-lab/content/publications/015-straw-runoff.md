## 中文摘要
本研究通过模拟降雨实验，探究了秸秆掺入耕作层对地表径流的影响。
### 研究亮点
- 模拟降雨条件下研究秸秆还田对径流的影响
- 秸秆掺入显著减少地表径流量
- 土壤入渗能力随秸秆掺入量增加而提高
- 为旱区雨水资源化利用提供技术支撑
## English Summary
This study explored the effect of straw incorporation into the farming soil layer on surface runoff under simulated rainfall.
### Highlights
- Studied straw incorporation effects on runoff under simulated rainfall
- Straw incorporation significantly reduced surface runoff
- Soil infiltration capacity increased with straw incorporation rate
- Provides technical support for rainwater utilization in dryland areas
