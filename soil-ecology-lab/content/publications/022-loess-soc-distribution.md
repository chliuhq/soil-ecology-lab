## 中文摘要
本研究分析了黄土丘陵区退耕还林小流域土壤有机碳的空间分布特征，并探讨了地形和植被因素的影响。
### 研究亮点
- 分析退耕小流域土壤有机碳空间分布特征
- 地形因子显著影响有机碳分布格局
- 植被恢复类型对碳储量有差异化影响
- 为退耕还林碳汇效益评估提供基础数据
## English Summary
This study analyzed the spatial distribution of soil organic carbon in a revegetated small watershed in the Loess Hilly Region and explored the influences of topography and vegetation.
### Highlights
- Analyzed SOC spatial distribution in a revegetated small watershed
- Topographic factors significantly influenced SOC distribution patterns
- Vegetation restoration types had differential effects on carbon stocks
- Provides baseline data for Grain-for-Green carbon sequestration assessment
