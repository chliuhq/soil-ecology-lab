## 中文摘要
本研究结合先进InSAR技术和Google Earth Engine机器学习方法，实现了区域黑土侵蚀的遥感监测。
### 研究亮点
- InSAR与GEE机器学习结合监测黑土侵蚀
- 以延寿县为案例验证方法有效性
- 实现了区域尺度黑土侵蚀定量监测
- 为东北黑土保护提供遥感技术方案
## English Summary
This study combined advanced InSAR techniques with machine learning in Google Earth Engine to monitor regional black soil erosion.
### Highlights
- Combined InSAR with GEE machine learning for black soil erosion monitoring
- Validated method effectiveness using Yanshou County case study
- Achieved regional-scale quantitative black soil erosion monitoring
- Provides remote sensing solutions for Northeast China black soil protection
