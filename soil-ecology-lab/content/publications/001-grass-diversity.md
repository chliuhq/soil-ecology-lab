## 中文摘要

本研究通过模拟极端降水实验，揭示了草本植物物种多样性对减少水土流失和养分损失的关键作用。研究发现，物种多样性越高，地表径流和养分流失越少，为生态系统应对极端气候事件提供了重要的科学依据。

### 研究亮点
- 构建1-8种草本植物多样性梯度，模拟极端降水事件
- 物种多样性显著降低地表径流量和泥沙流失量
- 高多样性群落减少了氮磷等养分的流失
- 互补效应是多样性减少水土流失的主要机制

## English Summary

This study reveals through simulated extreme precipitation experiments that grass species diversity plays a critical role in reducing water and nutrient loss. Higher species diversity significantly decreased surface runoff and nutrient losses, providing important scientific evidence for ecosystem resilience under extreme climate events.

### Highlights
- Constructed 1-8 species diversity gradients under simulated extreme precipitation
- Species diversity significantly reduced surface runoff and sediment loss
- High-diversity communities decreased nitrogen and phosphorus nutrient losses
- Complementarity effects are the primary mechanism for diversity-driven erosion reduction
