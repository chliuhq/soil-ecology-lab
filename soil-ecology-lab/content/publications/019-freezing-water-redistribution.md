## 中文摘要
本研究通过综述和全球荟萃分析，系统总结了冻结过程引起的土壤水分再分布规律。
### 研究亮点
- 综述与荟萃分析结合揭示冻结水分再分布规律
- 冻融循环驱动土壤水分迁移的机制解析
- 温度梯度和土壤质地是关键影响因素
- 为寒区水文模拟和水资源管理提供理论基础
## English Summary
This review and global meta-analysis systematically summarized patterns of freezing-induced soil water redistribution.
### Highlights
- Review and meta-analysis revealing freezing-induced water redistribution patterns
- Mechanism analysis of freeze-thaw driven soil water migration
- Temperature gradient and soil texture are key influencing factors
- Provides theoretical foundations for cold region hydrological simulation
