## 中文摘要
本研究利用机器学习方法建立了土壤热导率、含水量和基质势之间的定量关联。
### 研究亮点
- 机器学习建立土壤热-水参数的定量关联
- 模型有效揭示了热-水耦合的非线性关系
- 预测精度优于传统经验模型
- 为土壤物理参数预测提供新技术手段
## English Summary
This study used machine learning to establish quantitative connections between soil thermal conductivity, water content, and matric potential.
### Highlights
- ML established quantitative connections between soil thermal-hydraulic parameters
- Models effectively revealed nonlinear thermal-hydraulic coupling
- Prediction accuracy surpassed traditional empirical models
- Provides new technical approaches for soil physical parameter prediction
