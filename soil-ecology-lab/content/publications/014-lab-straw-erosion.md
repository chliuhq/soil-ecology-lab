## 中文摘要
本研究通过室内模拟实验，研究了小麦秸秆掺入对耕作坡面土壤侵蚀的影响。
### 研究亮点
- 室内模拟不同秸秆掺入量对坡面侵蚀的影响
- 秸秆还田有效减少了坡面侵蚀量
- 秸秆改善了土壤团聚体稳定性和抗蚀性
- 为农田水土保持措施优化提供实验依据
## English Summary
This study investigated the effects of wheat straw incorporation on soil erosion of cultivated slopes through laboratory simulation.
### Highlights
- Lab simulation of different straw incorporation rates on slope erosion
- Straw incorporation effectively reduced slope erosion
- Straw improved soil aggregate stability and erosion resistance
- Provides experimental evidence for optimizing farmland conservation measures
