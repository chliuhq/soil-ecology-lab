## 个人简介 / Bio

主要关注植物多样性与土壤水碳耦合关系、土壤有机碳形成与转化机制和土壤侵蚀与水土保持等方向。综合采用公共数据库、荟萃（meta）分析和野外以及室内实验等方法，开展全球、区域和样地尺度的土壤生态学、森林生态系统和生态水文等方向的研究。

---

My research focuses on plant diversity–soil water-carbon coupling, soil organic carbon formation and transformation mechanisms, and soil erosion and conservation. I integrate public databases, meta-analysis, field observations, and laboratory experiments to conduct multi-scale research in soil ecology, forest ecosystems, and ecohydrology.
