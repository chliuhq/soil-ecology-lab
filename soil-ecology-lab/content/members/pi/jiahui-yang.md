## 个人简介 / Bio

主要研究方向为多源遥感定量监测、热融灾害时空分布特征、水土保持与气候变化、土壤侵蚀过程与机理。

---

My research focuses on multi-source remote sensing quantitative monitoring, spatiotemporal distribution of thaw-induced hazards, soil and water conservation under climate change, and soil erosion processes and mechanisms.
